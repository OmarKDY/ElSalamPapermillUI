From Legacy to Innovation: Redefining Paper Manufacturing for a Sustainable Future!

El Salam Paper Mill, established in 1970 in Alexandria, Egypt, has a rich history as an independent family business. This foundation has been instrumental in our growth and success, allowing us to evolve into a state-of-the-art paper mill renowned for its innovative board solutions.

With our strategically located facility, we proudly serve customers worldwide, catering to their diverse needs. Our product portfolio encompasses a wide range of offerings, including traditional Coated and Uncoated board, as well as vibrant grades of high-quality Bristol and Manilla Boards. Guided by the foresight of our management and an unwavering commitment to the development of new paper applications, El Salam remains at the forefront of the board industry. Our dedication to innovation and excellence has solidified our position as a leading company, shaping the industry's landscape

Our mission

Our mission

Commitment to Sustainability

At El Salam Paper Mill, we are deeply committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. Leveraging our state-of-the-art technology, we strive to minimize the ecological impact of the paper industry by promoting the efficient use of resources, reducing waste, and maximizing the recycling and reuse of paper materials.

Preserving the Environment

Our advanced technology enables us to transform recycled paper bills into high-quality boards with exceptional precision and efficiency. By incorporating cutting-edge processes and equipment, we enhance our ability to preserve and protect the environment, ensuring that every step of our production aligns with sustainable practices.

Advancing Circular Economy

Through our advanced technology, we optimize the recycling process and elevate the quality of our board solutions. This empowers us to play a significant role in advancing the principles of a circular economy, where recycled paper materials are reintroduced into the production cycle, minimizing waste and maximizing resource utilization.

Customer-Centric Approach

With our advanced technology, we can offer customers an extensive range of customizable board solutions that meet their exact specifications. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures consistent quality, superior performance, and precise manufacturing processes, enabling us to deliver sustainable board solutions tailored to our customers' needs.

Innovation and Continuous Improvement

Our investment in advanced technology reflects our commitment to innovation and continuous improvement. By leveraging the latest advancements in the industry, we enhance our manufacturing capabilities, optimize efficiency, and explore new possibilities to further reduce our environmental footprint and drive sustainability in the paper bill recycling process.

Industry Leadership

Equipped with our advanced technology, we are proud to be an industry leader in sustainable paper manufacturing. By consistently pushing the boundaries of technological innovation, we set new benchmarks for environmental stewardship, serving as a role model for the industry and inspiring others to adopt similar advanced technologies in their operations.
